Keyboardist, Composer and Producer - Sid Acharya is a 19 year old musician working on the fusion of Eastern and Western sounds with a unique splash of Electronic resonance.

He pursues a Music Performance Degree at Holland College, Canada on a pathway program to the Berklee School of Music, Boston. Born in Chennai, India, his childhood led to an exposure of a myriad of styles. This has furnished him with an unparalleled ability to adapt and evolve as a composer, producer and performer. Caught in the evolving process of exploring and building his own sound, his style is an attempt to unify those of Indian classical, jazz and world music.

Sid Acharya creates a unique electronic sound, where his Indian classical roots fuse vibrantly with his ambient, jazz and world music explorations.
